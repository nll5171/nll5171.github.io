Vault i

Vault i was a mod of Fallout: New Vegas created during my 2nd year of RIT’s Game Design & Development program. It was a group project created in a team of 5 for the Game Modding class I took during the latter half of the academic year.

Our goal for this class was to develop a custom quest for a game of our choice, in which the player would navigate a custom area and receive a custom weapon as a reward. This quest was chosen to be immediately outside the starting area for easy access, and involved following a strange man’s reports about the existence of a cryptid nearby.

This would send the player to uncover a mysterious vault that had become home to a cult of individuals wearing animal skins and severed animal heads, with a super mutant as the leader. Despite initial plans to make a more involved quest, we chose to simplify things into simply fighting and disposing of the cult and its leader in combat.

Defeating the super mutant would give you their severed head as an item which you could present to the strange man and receive an extra powerful shotgun from the man, using custom textures and stats.

I was in charge of developing the costumes for the cultists, which saw me repurpose and retexture several in-game assets into new headgear and clothing, as well as the super mutant’s severed head. I also handled some additional scripting during the quest, and the injection of character voices.
