Picross

How to Play

This is a puzzle game in which you fill in blocks in a grid based on the numbers on each row and column to make a picture.

Each number indicates the amount of blocks to fill in consecutively in that row or column. Spaces between numbers mean that there is a gap of at least 1 block between the filled-in blocks.

Left click to fill-in a block. Left click again to undo.

You can also right click to mark blocks that you don't fill in. These will be colored gray.

Once complete, the final picture will be shown!
