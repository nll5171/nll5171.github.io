JoJo's and Damage Calculation

This video was designed as an in-depth exploration into, and analysis of, the systems behind damage calculation in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Heritage for the Future. Much like with the Infinite Prevention System, the details were not fully known, though there was more to go off of compared to the former. Nevertheless, there were many more systems in play and the code analysis and research took far longer.

I can no longer remember how long it took to research everything, but I'm grateful that I at least had the sense to migrate the notes I took into a Google Document after I finished research, which can be found here.

In any case, the research was finished by May 2022, though I experienced a terrible writer's block that kept me from finishing the script until early November. Then, after my university semester had finished, I rushed to get the project out before the year's end, and managed to do so on the last day of the year, December 31st, 2022.

This video was a definite step up in scale, and I'm still very proud of my ability to replicate the Sam & Max: Beyond Time and Space intro using sprites from Heritage for the Future. It was arguably the longest project I ever finished, though I've since procrastinated on another video that, if finished, would have taken much much longer to get done.
