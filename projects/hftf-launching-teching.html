Launching and Techning in JoJo's

Following the success and, more importantly, the fun time I had with making JoJo's and Infinite Prevention, I started work on a much smaller scale video regarding how launching attacks and air recoveries work in JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Heritage for the Future

Since it was a much shorter project, I was able to start research in mid-late November and, despite delays from holiday plans, finished a month or so later. Writing the script only took 4 days, and I had finished editing a week later.

To be honest, I often forget I made this video, but it's no less useful, even if very small in scale compared to the last video and videos to come.
