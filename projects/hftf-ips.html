Heritage for the Future: Rainbow Edition

This video was designed as an exploration into, and explanation of, the Infinite Prevention System used by JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Heritage for the Future. Said system is intended to prevent infinite combos, though it unfortunately wasn't entirely successful at it. Morover, the system was fairly inconsistent, to the point where no-one truly understood how it worked. I wanted to understand how it worked, and so I began investigating the process.

If memory serves, I began exploring the code behind it during the tail end of the summer of 2021, after having finished the Roman Cancel Romhack. I traced through instructions via Mame Debugger and recorded notes in Notepad++ (though I've unfortunately lost said notes on the matter).

Research was completed and the script writing began in September. I finished writing said script at the start of October and finished editing two weeks later.

This project was my first time making a heavily edited video since Sophomore year of high school, and it took quite a bit to get things down properly. Nonetheless, I'm pretty happy with how it turned out.
