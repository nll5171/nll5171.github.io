Heritage for the Future: Rainbow Edition

Rainbow edition is a romhack I created in Winter of 2020 for the Heritage for Our Future charity event that was hosted to help raise money for St. Jude’s Children’s Research Hospital. Rainbow Edition is an unofficial hack of Street Fighter II: Champion Edition that adds all sorts of absurd changes that radically alter the game.

The goal of this romhack was to achieve a similar effect through making a wide variety of different, comically absurd changes to characters, alongside having them start with max resources. I was responsible for handling all the gameplay changes to the systems of the game, as well as individual characters, while another collaborator made the adjustments to the sound and music.

At its core, the romhack was never designed to be a serious experience, and found itself victim to numerous inescapable checkmate situations as a result. Despite this, it achieved the goal I had set out to fulfill, and it made for an enjoyable experience for those wanting to play it, and those spectating it as well. Like the Roman Cancel romhack before it, this was also added to FightCade, the software client for playing arcade titles online.

Attached below is a tweet containing the changes made in this romhack.
